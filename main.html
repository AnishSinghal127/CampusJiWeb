Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book the basketball court?

+
You can book the basketball court through the Services section. Navigate to Court Booking System and select your preferred time slot.

Where can I report a lost item?

+
Visit the Services section and click on Lost and Found Center.

How do I track my parcel delivery?

+
Go to Services > Parcel Management Module to track your deliveries.

Can I submit grievances anonymously?

+
Yes, the Grievance Redressal System allows anonymous submissions.

How do I join clubs and committees?

+
Check the Clubs and Committees section for updated profiles of student bodies.

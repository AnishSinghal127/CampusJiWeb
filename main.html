Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

How do I book the basketball court?

+
You can book the basketball court through the Services section. Navigate to Court Booking System and select your preferred time slot. Bookings are prioritized based on requirement and availability.

Where can I report a lost item?

+
Visit the Services section and click on Lost and Found Center. You can report lost items with photos and description, or check if your item has been found by others.

How do I track my parcel delivery?

+
Go to Services > Parcel Management Module to track your deliveries. You'll receive real-time updates on parcels received at the parcel room and pickup instructions.

Can I submit grievances anonymously?

+
Yes, the Grievance Redressal System allows anonymous submissions. You can track responses and see the status of all grievances on the platform while maintaining confidentiality.

How do I join clubs and committees?

+
Check the Clubs and Committees section for updated profiles of student bodies, upcoming events, and contact details. You can reach out to the respective coordinators for membership information.
